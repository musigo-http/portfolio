MYB

MYB (Meet Your B2B) is a new project, i created MYB for facilitate the collaboration, based in the match like tinder you can swipe in the right for match a profile, after this you can make a collaboration with this profile

with MYB you can collaborate with any company only with simple swipe or click in the connect button

i started this project in june 2025

you can see the source code of MYB in github: https://github.com/musigo-http/myb
